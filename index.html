ABOUT THE CAFE

We Believe in the Pursuit of Doing Good. As it has been from the beginning, our goal is much greater than making money. We think "My Cafe" could and ought to make a difference in the communities we serve.

We provide fresh, made-to-order breakfast and lunch sandwiches along with a variety of sides, salads, and other delicious items in addition to our extensive espresso and brew bar menu.
